REVIEW

Alex Rea February 9, 2023

I love The Walking Dead! This show is right up my ally, as I love zombies, gore, and thrillers. Following the show through it's many seasons, I've grown very fond of a lot of the cast. I love the elements of suspense, shock, and mystery that surround this show. Overall I'd give this show a 10/10 without a doubt!

The actors/actresses play they're role amazingly. Andrew Lincoln, Norman Reedus, and Jeffrey Dean Morgan have become some of my favorite actors after watching this masterpiece. My favorite character being Negan, who is played by Jeffrey Dean Morgan.
